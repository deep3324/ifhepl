About Us

Since our company's establishment in the year 2016, we have continued to expand organically in the health, agriculture, finance, and education sector with the help of dynamic communities, clubs, and services. We currently operate in Karnataka, Odisha, Punjab, West Bengal, Madhya Pradesh, Jharkhand, Uttar Pradesh, and Bihar and are working incessantly on expanding to the other states.

We at IFHE Pvt. Ltd aim to empower every village man, woman, and child in ways that make them robust, healthier, and financially independent by bringing them access to all the essential amenities at their convenience that would help them focus on important parts of their life and enjoy the socio-cultural developments in the community, without having to worry over other trivial things.

We offer our services in various fields which primarily revolve around health, farming, finance, and the education sector. We for instance offer Health Card, Kisan Card, and Membership Card. These cards bring in all the benefits and services available around the states, but for a relatively cheaper and generous price point than the rest of the industry, which any person with very low sources of income can easily afford. The mission and vision of our company are to ultimately enable you such that you can create different opportunities for yourself instead of waiting indefinitely for them.

IFHE Pvt. Ltd has always been very encouraging of the development of the community for farmers in every way possible. These clubs, in many years of operations, have been tested over time and have turned out to be a very strong driving force in the village projects. These clubs are where farmers from all the areas visit and discuss various day-to-day activities to be performed in the community. They have also become a source of financial security for the community, a safety net to which they can fall in any case of adversity.

Our company collaborates with various agricultural societies to bring the best scientific knowledge of crop plantation and provide regular guidance till the rearing is done. We constantly keep in touch with various farmer groups to improve yields by applying the best practices in agriculture.

With our various community activities and services, we aim to establish various clubs which would help break every kind of social barrier and dismantle the patriarchal caste divisions, therefore, helping grow the village economy and the mutual growth which originates from their success.

VISION:

At IFHE Pvt. Ltd, we have set our mission to establish ecologically and economically sustainable models of agriculture, help farmers to organize themselves to improve their livelihoods. To empower them with the right tools and the digital services that can help them ease their life and get connected globally to the world and decipher the power of technology and realize their rights and where they stand with the fast pacing world. Our mission is to make every person financially independent by bringing down more useful courses to learn new skills, at the bare minimum than the industry so that it is affordable to each and every individual easily. These added skills can always pave the way to new paths of income and help improve living standards.

MISSION:

We have plans to reach out to 50 thousand farmers directly and 5 lakh farmers indirectly in the next five years (2021-2026), by working with them to establish sustainable models in agriculture and fair marketing models in six states of India. By doing so, we believe that agriculture productivity can be increased by 10 % and farmers’ incomes by 15 % which approximately translates to Rs. 5000 per family.

To improve the quality of life of farming communities by promoting environmentally safe and sustainable methods that would enhance the quality and quantity of crop yields especially in tribal and dryland areas.

To enhance the participation of farmers, both women and men, in all processes of problem analysis, technology development, evaluation, adoption, and extension leading to food security and self-reliance among farmers and rural communities.

To facilitate community access and control over natural resources and to build institutions and coalitions at different levels for strengthening their bargaining power with markets and governments, which focus on empowerment of marginalized sections like Women, Dalits, Adivasis, and Minorities.

To develop a Sustainable Agriculture Resource Network that promotes sharing of knowledge, material, and human resources. To serve as a repository for documenting, collecting, storing, collating, and disseminating information/success stories from different sources on sustainable agriculture.

Understand the role of agriculture in contributing to climate change and developing sustainable agriculture practices which can help in mitigating and adapting to climate change.

Establish and incubate Farmer Producer Organisations, Community Enterprises and promote entrepreneurial skills in rural areas.
